关于 Catalan 数的常见公式：

$$
H_n = \begin{cases}
    \sum_{i=1}^{n} H_{i-1} H_{n-i} & n \geq 2, n \in \mathbf{N_{+}}\\
    1 & n = 0, 1
\end{cases}
$$

$$
H_n = \frac{H_{n-1} (4n-2)}{n+1}
$$

$$
H_n = \binom{2n}{n} - \binom{2n}{n-1}
$$

$$
H_n = \frac{\binom{2n}{n}}{n+1}(n \geq 2, n \in \mathbf{N_{+}})
$$
